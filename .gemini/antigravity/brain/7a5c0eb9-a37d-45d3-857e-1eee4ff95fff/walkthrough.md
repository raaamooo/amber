# Animation & Styling Enhancements Complete

Both versions of the restaurant menu have received significant visual upgrades! The interactions are now much more dynamic and premium.

## Bites & Sips (Modern)
- **3D Card Reveals:** As you scroll down the page, the menu cards no longer just fade in; they tilt into view on a 3D axis with a heavier, spring-like easing.
- **Card Hovers:** Hovering over a card now produces a multi-layered soft drop shadow and slightly scales up the whole card, while the image itself subtly zooms in for a premium effect.
- **Bouncy Flips:** The "Play With Us" recommendation card now flips with a bouncing physics effect rather than a stiff, linear turn.
- **Smooth Navigation:** The hamburger menu lines now gracefully transform into a perfect 'X' with a bouncy cubic-bezier transition.

## Anubis' Table (Ancient)
- **Deep Stone Aesthetics:** Menu cards now feature a heavy inset shadow, making them look deeply carved into stone rather than sitting flat on the page.
- **Sepia Hover Effect:** All food images are given a moody sepia/dark filter by default. When you hover over a card, the image slowly zooms in and restores its full color and brightness, creating an immersive reveal.
- **Glowing Torches:** The main gold buttons now continuously pulse with a soft golden glow even when you aren't hovering over them.
- **Dynamic Elements:** The floating background hieroglyphs now fade in and out asynchronously (at different speeds and times), making the background feel more alive and less repetitive.
- **Heavy Tomb Drop:** The recommendation scroll in "The Trial of Anubis" now drops into view like a heavy stone slab, crashing down with a deep 3D perspective tilt.
- **Dynamic Sand:** The intro animation's sand particles now drift diagonally, scaling up and down as they move to create a 3D depth effect.

All changes have been successfully implemented across both directories!
